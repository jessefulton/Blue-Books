Occupy Blue Books

Huh?

Fees and tuition for public higher education in the state of California have been increasing at record rates. Last Spring, in response to the unjustifiable fee increases, a UCSC student* created a print of a modified University of California seal addressing the issue, subtly replacing the motto of "Let There Be Light" with "Let There Be Fees"

I couldn't let such a great idea die. So, I appropriated the logo and printed it onto about 25 "blue books" (examination books.) After (re-)printing the blue books, I planted the hacked exam books amongst the standard blue books for sale in UCSC's Bay Tree book store.

*If anybody knows this artist, please let me know.

occupy & remix

This project can't die here. It needs to continue. Occupy this hack. Download it. Remix it. Reprint it.

Take this project. Download the hacked blue book Photoshop template (right-click and "save as") and follow the directions in the video above to create your own hacked blue books. Print them up, hand them out, plant them somewhere - do anything.

Not from a UC school? Hack your own blue books and contribute them back to this project at github. This project is open source and anybody can contribute to it.
